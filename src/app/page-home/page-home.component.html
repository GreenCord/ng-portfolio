Steve Martinez – Web Developer

My career began as a content specialist building HTML content on websites, later taking on a position to manage the entire website for PPG's automotive refinish business unit as an Interactive Marketing Specialist.

Over time, I incorporated a number of various digital marketing concepts into the content that our website supported, in addition to creating an email marketing program and social media channels to round out our digital marketing portfolio.

Eventually, I supervised a team of three digital marketing coordinators, together managing all of the content projects and day to day content updates and messaging for the business unit.

Growing Interest in Development

In 2014, we relaunched a redesigned website in the company's new Kentico software installation, and I began learning many aspects of development using Kentico's portal engine to design and develop templates using Kentico's own macro language (K#, a variant of C#).

Over time, I have programmed and built additional websites, including the Vibrance Collection® brand website, the Refinish Brand Assets website, and a website dedicated to support the launch of the business's next generation color matching hardware and software.

Future Career in Development

In order to further my career and branch out into full stack development, I've completed a coding bootcamp hosted at Case Western Reserve Univeristy in Cleveland, OH—as well as continuing my learning with online coding courses.
